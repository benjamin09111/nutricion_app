# NutriNet Product Context

NutriNet is a premium SaaS platform designed specifically for Nutritionists in Chile. Its mission is to transform the clinical workflow from a manual, time-consuming process into an automated, AI-enhanced experience.

## 1. Product Vision & Mission
- **Target**: Professional Nutritionists (clinical and freelance).
- **Goal**: Become the daily tool of choice for nutritionists by saving them massive amounts of time and automating repetitive tasks.
- **Core Strategy**: Solve the "local pain" (Chilean market) through market intelligence, automated calculations, and premium UX.

## 2. Key Value Propositions
- **Time Saving**: Automate the most tedious parts of the job (calculations, document generation).
- **Process Automation**: From patient management to the final delivery of the clinical plan.
- **Precision Calculations**: Eliminate human error in nutritional math.
- **Fast Personalization**: Deliver highly personalized diet and nutrition plans faster than any manual method.

## 3. Core Features (Current & Near Future)
- **Patient Management (CRM)**: Simple and efficient tracking of history, appointments, and progress.
- **Automated Diet Engine**: Constraint-based planning with real-time nutritional feedback.
- **Dish Generator (AI)**: Using AI to create practical, creative, and nutritionally balanced recipes/dishes.
- **The 4-Stage Engine**: 
    1. **Dieta**: Strategic planning.
    2. **Recetas y Porciones**: Practical quantification.
    3. **Carrito**: Automated shopping list.
    4. **Entregable**: Professional PDF export for the patient.

## 4. Design & Brand Identity
- **Visual Vibe**: Clean, simple, friendly, and energetic.
- **Theme**: Strictly **Light Mode** using the established color palette (Indigo/Green/Ivory).
- **Mascot**: The **Otter (Nutria)**. She is our energetic helper, designed to make the platform feel alive and supportive during the nutritionist's daily work.
- **Daily Usage focus**: The UI must feel comfortable for "all-day" use, minimizing cognitive load and friction.

## 5. Strategic Roadmap
- **Initial Focus**: Nutrition and Dietetics.
- **Retention**: Focus on making the "Day-to-Day" workflow so smooth that the monthly subscription becomes indispensable.
- **AI Expansion**: Beyond dish generation, AI will gradually assist in more strategic decision-making and patient interaction.
